BMIcare Calculator App

The BMIcare calculator app is a web-based tool designed to calculate Body Mass Index (BMI) based on user input of height and weight. BMI is a measure used to assess body weight relative to height,and it is commonly used as an indicator of overall health and risk of certain diseases
